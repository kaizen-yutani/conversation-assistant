# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2024**

## Overview

Conversation Assistant ("the App") is designed with privacy as a core principle. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data We Collect

### Screenshots (When You Request Analysis)

- **What**: Screenshots you capture using the app
- **When**: Only when you press the Analyze button (⌘Enter)
- **Where**: Sent to Anthropic's Claude AI service
- **Storage**: Not stored by our app; Anthropic's retention policy applies
- **Purpose**: To provide AI-powered analysis of coding problems

### Notes (Stored Locally)

- **What**: Text you write in the Notes tab
- **Where**: Stored locally on your Mac using UserDefaults
- **Storage**: Persists until you delete it
- **Sharing**: Never sent anywhere; stays on your device

### API Keys (Stored Locally)

- **What**: Your Anthropic and OpenAI API keys
- **Where**: Stored securely in macOS Keychain
- **Sharing**: Only sent to the respective API services for authentication

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- ❌ Personal information (name, email, etc.)
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Usage analytics or telemetry
- ❌ Crash reports
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ Contacts or calendar data

## Third-Party Services

### Anthropic (Claude AI)

When you analyze screenshots, they are sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data:
- https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

### OpenAI (Optional)

If you configure an OpenAI API key for voice features (future), audio would be sent to OpenAI. Their privacy policy applies:
- https://openai.com/privacy

## Data Security

- **API Keys**: Stored in macOS Keychain with hardware encryption
- **Notes**: Stored in app sandbox, inaccessible to other apps
- **Network**: All API calls use HTTPS encryption
- **No Cloud Sync**: Your data never touches our servers

## Your Rights

### Access & Deletion

- **Notes**: View and delete anytime within the app
- **API Keys**: Clear via Settings > Clear Keys
- **Consent**: Revoke data sharing consent by not using the Analyze feature

### Data Portability

- Export your notes as Markdown via File > Export Notes

## Children's Privacy

Conversation Assistant is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.
